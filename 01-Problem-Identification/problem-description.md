# Problem Identification

## Background of the Organization
Children’s homes play a vital role in providing care, protection, and educational support for children in need. However, many such organizations still rely on manual methods such as paper records and spreadsheets to manage daily operations.

## Identified Issue
Manual data management creates several challenges:
- Difficulty in accessing and updating records
- Risk of data loss and errors
- Time-consuming report generation
- Lack of centralized information

Important data such as attendance, medical history, and donations are stored separately, making management inefficient.

## Stakeholder Analysis
Primary stakeholders include:
- Administrators: Manage operations, records, and reports
- Staff Members: Handle daily activities such as attendance tracking
- Children: Indirect beneficiaries of improved system efficiency

## Justification of Proposed Solution
A centralized digital system will:
- Reduce dependency on manual processes
- Improve accuracy and accessibility of data
- Enable efficient report generation
- Support better decision-making

This system addresses a real-world problem while improving operational efficiency in children’s homes.
