# CareNest System  
## Children’s Home Management and Support System

---

## Introduction

Children’s homes manage important information related to children's wellbeing, including attendance, medical records, and donations. Many organizations still rely on manual systems such as paper records and spreadsheets, which makes managing data difficult and time-consuming.

CareNest is a web-based Children’s Home Management and Support System designed to help administrators manage children’s information, monitor daily activities, and generate reports efficiently through a centralized digital platform.

---

## Problem Statement

Children’s homes manage a large amount of information about children. Many homes still rely on manual systems such as paper files or spreadsheets. As a result:

- Data is stored in different places  
- It is difficult to access information quickly  
- There is a risk of data loss and errors  
- Report generation is slow and inefficient  

These issues highlight the need for a centralized digital solution.

---

## Project Purpose

The system aims to improve efficiency and organization within children’s homes by:

- Providing a centralized system for managing records  
- Supporting attendance tracking and medical record management  
- Improving transparency in donation management  
- Reducing manual workload  

---

## Project Objectives

- Develop a centralized web-based system  
- Reduce dependency on paper-based records  
- Improve accessibility and accuracy of data  
- Support monitoring of children's health and activities  
- Enable report generation for decision-making  

---

## System Scope (Initial)

The system was initially designed to include multiple modules:

- Attendance Management System  
- Child Monitoring and Report Generation System  
- Meal Management System  
- Donation Management System  
- Medical Records Management System  

These ,modules were intended to work together to manage different operational areas of the children’s home.

---

## Scope Reduction and Refinement

During the later stages of the project, it became necessary to refine the system scope due to time constraints and overlapping academic workload.

The project was adjusted to focus on **core and high-impact modules**:

- Child Management System  
- Attendance Management System  
- Medical Records Management System  

This refinement allowed the team to:

- Focus on essential system functionalities  
- Ensure quality over quantity  
- Develop a working and complete prototype within the given timeframe  

Other modules such as Meal Management and Donation Management were excluded from implementation but remain part of the overall system design for future expansion.

---

## System Overview

The refined system provides a centralized platform where administrators and staff can:

- Manage child information  
- Track attendance records  
- Maintain medical records  
- View system summaries through dashboards  

---

## Development Methodology

The project follows the **Waterfall Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)**, where each phase is completed sequentially:

1. Requirement Analysis  
2. System Design  
3. Implementation  
4. Testing  
5. Deployment  

This approach was selected due to clearly defined requirements and a structured timeline.

---

## Summary

CareNest is designed as a practical solution to improve the management of children’s homes by digitizing core operations. The system focuses on usability, efficiency, and structured data management, providing a strong foundation for future expansion.
