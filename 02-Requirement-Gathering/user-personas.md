# User Personas

## 1. Administrator Persona

**Name:** Mrs. Perera  
**Age:** 60  
**Role:** Administrator of the Children’s Home  

**Background:**
Mrs. Perera is responsible for managing daily operations of the children’s home. She handles records related to children, attendance, medical information, and reporting. She has limited experience with digital systems and is more comfortable with manual record-keeping.

**Goals:**
- Maintain accurate records of children
- Easily access medical and attendance information
- Generate reports without manual effort

**Challenges:**
- Limited familiarity with technology
- Difficulty managing large amounts of paper-based data
- Time-consuming manual processes

**Needs:**
- A simple and easy-to-use interface
- Clear navigation and minimal complexity
- System guidance or training support

---

## 2. Staff Persona

**Name:** Staff Member (General)  
**Age:** 30–45  
**Role:** Caregiver / Staff Member  

**Background:**
Staff members are responsible for daily operational tasks such as monitoring children, marking attendance, and assisting in routine activities. They have basic familiarity with smartphones or simple digital systems but are not highly technical.

**Goals:**
- Quickly record daily attendance
- Access basic child information when needed
- Perform tasks efficiently with minimal steps

**Challenges:**
- Limited time for administrative tasks
- Need for quick and simple interactions
- Avoiding errors while recording data

**Needs:**
- Fast and responsive system
- Simple input methods (e.g., checkboxes, toggles)
- Clear and readable interface

---

## 3. Indirect User (Children)

**Name:** Children (Residents)  
**Age:** Varies  

**Role:** Beneficiaries of the system  

**Background:**
Children living in the home are not direct users of the system. However, their data such as attendance, medical records, and progress are managed through the system.

**Goals:**
- Receive proper care and monitoring
- Ensure their information is accurately recorded

**Needs:**
- Accurate and well-maintained records
- Timely medical care and attention
