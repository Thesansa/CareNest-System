# Requirement Gathering

## Interview Summary
Stakeholder discussions revealed that current systems rely heavily on manual processes. Administrators highlighted difficulties in managing attendance, medical records, and generating reports efficiently.

## User Personas

### Admin
- Manages child records, medical data, and reports
- Needs quick access to accurate information

### Staff
- Records daily attendance
- Requires a simple and efficient interface

---

## User Stories

### Attendance Management
- As a staff member, I want to mark attendance so that children’s presence is tracked
- As an admin, I want to view attendance reports

### Medical Management
- As an admin, I want to create medical records for children
- As an admin, I want to track treatments and vaccinations

---

## Functional Requirements
- Register and manage child information
- Record and view attendance
- Manage medical records
- Generate reports

---

## Non-Functional Requirements
- System should be user-friendly
- Data should be stored securely
- System should be reliable and efficient
- Fast response time for data retrieval
