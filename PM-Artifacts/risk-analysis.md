# Risk Analysis

## Overview
This section identifies potential risks that may affect the development and success of the CareNest system, along with mitigation strategies.

---

## Risk Table

| Risk ID | Risk Description                          | Impact  | Likelihood | Mitigation Strategy |
|--------|------------------------------------------|--------|------------|----------------------|
| R1     | Limited technical knowledge of users     | High   | High       | Design a simple and user-friendly interface and provide basic training |
| R2     | Delays due to academic workload          | High   | High       | Prioritize key features and manage time effectively |
| R3     | Lack of continuous stakeholder feedback  | Medium | Medium     | Conduct periodic reviews and validate requirements early |
| R4     | Data loss or mismanagement               | High   | Medium     | Use structured data handling and validation methods |
| R5     | Scope creep (adding unnecessary features)| Medium | Medium     | Focus only on core modules and defined requirements |
| R6     | Team coordination issues                 | Medium | Medium     | Maintain clear communication and task allocation |
| R7     | System usability issues                  | High   | Low        | Conduct usability testing and refine UI design |
